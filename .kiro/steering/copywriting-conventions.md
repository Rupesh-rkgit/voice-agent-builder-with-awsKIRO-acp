# Professional Copywriting Conventions

## Tone & Voice
- Professional, confident, and clear
- Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it
- No slang, emojis, or excessive exclamation marks
- Active voice over passive voice

## Email Best Practices
- Subject lines: 6-10 words, benefit-driven, no ALL CAPS
- Preview text: complement the subject line, don't repeat it
- Body: lead with value, one idea per paragraph, single primary CTA
- Sign-off: professional and consistent with brand

## Ad Copy Best Practices
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Include a clear, specific CTA ("Get your free trial" over "Click here")
- Respect platform limits (Google: 30/90 chars, Meta: 125 primary text)
- Use numbers and specifics over vague claims

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Spelling and grammar checked
- [ ] CTA is clear and actionable
- [ ] Tone matches audience expectations
- [ ] No compliance red flags
- [ ] Formatted for the target channel
